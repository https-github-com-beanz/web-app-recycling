Green Bin

Accepted Materials
  • Eggs and eggshells.
  • Tea and Teabags.
  • Lard, shortening, and fat.
  • Leftover cooking oils.
  • Branches and twigs.
  • Paper coffee cups.

Blue Bin

Accepted Materials
  • Empty glass.
  • Metals and plastic.
  • Aluminum foil.
  • Flower pots / planting trays.
  • Soft drink cans.
  • Aluminum containers.

Black Bin

Accepted Materials
  • Paper and cardboard.
  • Wrapping paper.
  • Paper bags.
  • Hard and soft cover books.
  • Clean pizza boxes.
  • Egg cartons.

Trash Bin

Accepted Materials
  • Liner bags.
  • Plastic bubble wrap.
  • Laminated plastic.
  • Candles and wax.
  • Cigarette butts and ashes.
  • Hot drink cups.
  • Cutlery.
  • Disposable mop sheets.
